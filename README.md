**#SUPERSTORE SALES DASHBOARD**



**Project Overview:**

This project analyzes sales and profitability using a sample superstore dataset from Kaggle. An interactive dashboard was built in Microsoft excel to provide insights into regional performance, product performance, customer segments, and monthly sales trends. The dashboard enables users to explore the data dynamically using slicers and a timeline. 



**Objectives:**

1\. Analyze sales performance across regions.

2\. Identify the most profitable product categories.

3\. Determine the best-selling products.

4\. Examine the monthly sales trends.

5\. Identify the highest-performing states by sales.



**Tools used:**

Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables

Pivot Charts

Slicers

Timelines

Excel Formulas



**Skills Demonstrated:**

Data Cleaning

Dashboard Design

Data Analysis

Data Visualization

Business Analysis



**Data Cleaning:**

Checked for duplicate record entries and confirmed that no duplicate entries were present.

Verified data types (dates, currencies, numbers etc).

Used conditional formatting to accentuate loss in profit (showing -negative entries in red)

Created calculated metrics where required.

Formatted numerical values for readability (K and M notation).



**Excel Formulas:**

Used SUM to calculate KPIs (Total sales and Total Profit).

Used COUNTA and UNIQUE in a nested formula to calculate the Total number(#) of Orders.

Derived COST using the relationship; *"Cost = Sales - Profit"* from the values given in the dataset.

Used Division (/) to calculate the Average Order Value.

**Key Insights:**

* The West region generated the highest sales
* Technology was the most profitable product category for the superstore
* Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced copier was the highest-selling product.
* Across the 2014 to 2017 period, sales generally peaked toward the end of the year, suggesting a recurring seasonal sales pattern.
* California recorded the highest number of sales among all states
* Consumers generated more revenue for the superstore compared to the other segments



**Recommendations:**

* Investigate why the south region trails other regions significantly in revenue
* Continue investing in high performing products and categories to generate more revenue
* Explore ways to generate more sales in low performing states like Idaho, New Mexico, Montana, Iowa etc.
* Increase inventory levels ahead of peak sales periods to meet anticipated demand.
* Develop targeted sales strategies for lower-performing categories such as Furniture.
